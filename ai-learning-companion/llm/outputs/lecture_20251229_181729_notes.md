**I. Introduction: What is an Algorithm?**

*   **Definition:** An algorithm is a step-by-step procedure for solving a computational problem. Think of it as a recipe for computers.
*   **Why it's Important:**
    *   **Core Subject:** Crucial for computer science engineering students.
    *   **Competitive Exams:** Frequently used in competitive exams (gate exams, programming contests).
    *   **Programming:** The foundation for writing programs.
*   **The Challenge:** Students often struggle with understanding algorithms because they are difficult to grasp initially.
*   **The Goal:** To provide a clear, step-by-step approach to solving problems.

**II. Key Definitions**

        *   Define the problem clearly.
        *   Create a design that is thorough and complete.
        *   Ensure the design is understandable.
2.  **Implementation Phase:**
    *   **Purpose:** Translate the design into a working program.
    *   **Steps:**
        *   Write the program.
        *   Start writing the program.

3.  **Algorithm Analysis:**
    *   **Purpose:** Evaluate the algorithm’s efficiency and correctness.
